## Closed-loop Focus

### Blind Listening Focus

Listen for the main information chain:

1. Japan has introduced or used a new word.
2. The word describes extremely hot days.
3. The standard is around 40 degrees Celsius or above.
4. The background is record-breaking heat in recent years.

### Dictation Focus

Pay special attention to:

- Numbers and temperatures
- Weather-related expressions
- Cause-and-effect expressions
- Public-topic vocabulary
- Names of institutions if they appear in the text

### Partial Recitation Focus

Prioritize reciting the part that explains:

- What the new word means
- Why the word is needed
- How extreme heat has changed daily life or public discussion
