## Card 01

- 骨架：The part about ... was interesting.
- 功能：评价 / 表达兴趣
- 场景：考试 / podcast / meeting
- 提示词：
  - podcast / sleep habits
  - meeting / customer feedback
- 我的句子：
  - The part about sleeping habits was interesting.
  - The part about meeting in the US was interesting.
- 英文问题：
  - “sleeping habits”可以理解，但高频搭配更自然的是“sleep habits”
  - “meeting in the US”语义太泛，不够具体，建议改成“meeting culture in the US”或“how meetings are run in the US”
- 保留版：
  - I found the part about sleep habits particularly interesting.
  - I found the part about meeting culture in the US particularly interesting.
- 高频替换：
  - What I found particularly interesting was the part about sleep habits.
  - I was especially interested in the part about how meetings are run in the US.
- 等级：S
